# How leaders reset market narrative

Growth moments demand clarity. The most effective leadership teams align internal stakeholders and external signals
before they scale communications.

## Signals to watch

- Shifts in buyer language and priorities
- Competitive moves that change perception
- Internal misalignment across executive teams
